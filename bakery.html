About Us

Evan's bake shop, founded in 1960, is a cherished family-owned bakery known for its unwavering commitment to quality, warm family-centered service, and a blend of tradition and innovation. We prioritize community engagement, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, all while offering freshly baked, authentic treats that bring joy to our extended family of customers.
